The Survival Game

With detailed graphics (simple graphics available on block version) and easy to use controls, this is a simple avoid coming in contact with the lava game and once you collect all the coins of that level, you're automatically ascended to the next level. You're given three lives for each level which get eventually harder and harder and your goal is to not fall in the lava while collecting all the coins that come across your way. After you have used up all your lives, you lose and are asked to exit or replay.

This game is a great way to improve your problem solving and goal orientation skills since you're made to focus on the task and only by playing with full concentration will you be able to solve the problem (i.e. avoid the lava) \and achieve your goal (i.e collecting all the coins) and finally make it to the end. Happy Playing !

Tic Tac Toe (Multiplayer)

Tic-tac-toe, is a game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3×3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a diagonal, horizontal, or vertical row is the winner. This user friendly virtual UI allows you to play this world famous classic game with the help of a beautiful color pallete and without the need to bring out paper and pencil. You can play this on the visually appealing interace made with html css and js or play the python version of a less appealing one with your friends!

If you lose or if there's a draw, it allows you to replay the game or exit it and you can continue playing this for hours and hours as it improves your critical thinking ability along with concentration and problem solving skills.

2048

This is one of my personal favourites and an extremely fun game suitable for all ages. 2048 is a single-player sliding block puzzle game. The game's objective is to slide numbered tiles on a grid to combine them to create a tile with the number 2048. However, one can continue to play the game after reaching the goal, creating tiles with larger numbers.

AI Pong (Single Player)

Pong is a table tennis–themed arcade sports video game, featuring simple two-dimensional graphics. Basically, it is a virtual version of table tennis. With easy to navigate features controls along with a user friendly interface, my game allows you to play against the Computer AI. The AI predicts the position of the ball the next time it approaches the player and aims to position itself such that the ball will bounce off its paddle as further away from the opposing player as possible.

Putting together the knowloedge of Artificial Intelligence, CS and Physics with some degree of required mathematical calculations required to formulate this kind of AI player, our virtual sportsperson is quite a good opponent for people without a huge amount of game skill. Happy Playing!

Sodoku Player

Sudoku is a logic-based, combinatorial number-placement puzzle. In classic sudoku, the objective is to fill a 9×9 grid with digits so that each column, each row, and each of the nine 3×3 subgrids that compose the grid contains all of the digits from 1 to 9. Choose your level and get a personalised sodoku puzzle based on just for that. Solve a sodoku puzzle with backtracking and get the solution to your sodoku.

With user friendly UI and warm colors along with easy navigation tools, this game is great for people of all ages and is a great way to improve your critical thinking, mathematical skills and problem solving abilities.
